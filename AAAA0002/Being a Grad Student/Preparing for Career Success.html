Week 3 Being a Grad Student

Preparing for Career Success

Aligning your academic choices with your long-term career goals and skill development is critical for success.

Clarifying Career Goals

Reflect on your motivations for pursuing graduate school. Are you aiming for a promotion, starting a new career path, or following a passion? Understanding your "why" will help you make informed decisions about your academic journey. 

Refer back to the Ready, Set, Goal! [content page] to guide this step. 

Career Services Offerings

Take advantage of the numerous opportunities provided by Career Services to advance your career or launch a new one. The Career Services office offers a variety of resources to help you move forward: 

  • Resume Review: Get personalized feedback to enhance your resume and make it stand out to potential employers. 
  • Interview Preparation: Practice your interview skills and receive tips to improve your performance. 
  • Virtual Job Fairs: Connect with employers and explore job opportunities from the comfort of your home. 
  • Specialized Job Pages: Access job listings tailored to your specific program and field of study.
  • Employer Connections: Benefit from access to employers that prioritize hiring APUS graduates. 

Don't wait! Find out how to get ready for your next steps by visiting the Career Services [content page] in the Your Support Network unit.
