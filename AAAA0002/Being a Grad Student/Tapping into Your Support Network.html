Week 3 Being a Grad Student

Tapping into Your Support Network

Navigating your academic journey can be both exciting and challenging. Fortunately, you are not alone. Your support network is a robust system designed to help you succeed, comprising various teams and departments dedicated to your well-being and academic growth. By tapping into this network, you can unlock numerous resources and opportunities that will enhance your educational experience. 

Faculty

Your faculty are your first line of support for academic questions in your courses.

Do not hesitate to contact your instructor with questions and requests for help. Faculty members are available to meet with you virtually during office hours and provide extra assistance when needed.

Reach out to your instructor at any time via the 1:1 Chat with Prof [PDF] discussion space. 

Student Services

APUS offers a host of student services, which are covered in detail in the Your Support Network unit, including Advising, Career Services, the Chaplain and other Mental Health and Wellness Resources, Student Life and Engagement, and more!

By actively interacting with these teams and departments, you can create a strong support network that will help you thrive academically, personally, and professionally. Remember, reaching out for support is a sign of strength and a proactive step toward achieving your goals. Tap into these valuable resources—they are here for you!
