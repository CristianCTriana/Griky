Using Academic Resources Strategically

Navigating the academic landscape can be challenging, but leveraging the right resources can make a significant difference in your success. Here are some key resources you should consider.

The Success Center

The Success Center is a repository of curated resources designed to support you in achieving your academic goals. It includes a range of topics around academic success, health and wellness, technical tips and resources, and much more. 

Fun Fact: Resources from the Success Center are linked throughout this orientation course. 

For direct access, follow these steps: 

  1. Log in to your ecampus.
  2. Along the dark blue top navigation bar, select “SUCCESS CENTER” as shown in this screenshot:

    3. Figure 1: Screenshot of the Success Center link at the top of the ecampus homepage.

Tutor.com

Tutor.com offers 24/7 online tutoring that you can access anytime, from anywhere. Whether you need help with a specific subject, writing support, or general study strategies, you will be connected with experienced tutors who can provide personalized guidance. 

All APUS students receive 10 free hours of tutoring. This flexible resource is especially valuable for those needing support outside of regular class hours. Tutors can assist with writing, proofreading, statistics, ESL support, and more, making this a versatile tool for learners at every level. 

Access the Tutor.com [webpage] to learn more. 

Academic Advisors

Your Academic Advising team is a strategic ally in your educational journey. They can help you plan your course schedule, ensure you meet graduation requirements, and guide you in selecting your academic and career goals. Regular meetings with your advisor can keep you on track and help you make informed decisions about your education.

Learn more on the Academic Advising [content page] in the Your Support Network unit.

Career Coaching

Career coaches play a pivotal role in helping you bridge the gap between your academic achievements and your professional aspirations. They offer a range of services designed to help you align your educational pursuits with your career goals. 

Regular sessions with Career Services can ensure that you are well prepared to transition from academia to the workforce, making your academic journey more purposeful and goal-oriented. 

Additionally, this team manages the APUS Career Link platform, which houses additional career resources. 

Learn more on the Career Services [content page] in the Your Support Network unit.

The Library

The APUS Trefry Library is referenced throughout this orientation. It is a vital academic resource for all APUS students and will be one of your most powerful allies in your academic journey.  

The library is more than just a place to find books. It offers access to a wealth of academic resources, including research databases, journals, and multimedia materials. Librarians are available to assist with research projects and can help you locate the information you need. Taking advantage of the library's resources can enhance your research skills and provide you with the information necessary to excel in your studies. 

Whether you are conducting original research, writing a thesis, or simply looking for credible sources, the library offers a rich set of tools, services, and expert guidance to support your academic endeavors. 

A detailed overview of library services and resources for graduate students can be found on the Library page [content page] in the Your Support Network unit. A few highlights are mentioned here: 

  • Research Support : The Trefry Library offers extensive support to help you navigate the complexities of academic research. From basic search strategies to advanced methodologies, the library's resources are designed to enhance your skills and ensure you have access to the information you need. 
  • Writing Support: Strong writing skills go hand-in-hand with academic success, and the library provides a range of resources to support your development as a scholarly writer. Whether you need help learning to structure arguments, integrate sources, or maintain an academic tone, its writing resources are here to guide you. 
  • Specialized Databases:  The library provides access to a wide array of specialized databases, ensuring you have the most relevant and up-to-date information for your field of study. These databases are invaluable for conducting thorough and credible research.

To fully appreciate what the Trefry Library has to offer, you are encouraged to review the Library module in its entirety. This will give you a deeper understanding of the resources available and how they can enhance your academic experience. The library is here to empower you with the tools and support you need to succeed.

By strategically using these academic resources, you can enhance your learning experience, overcome challenges, and achieve your academic goals. Remember, seeking help and utilizing available resources is a smart strategy for success.
