Week 7 - Knowledge Check

Question 1: What is the primary difference between traditional Agile and Distributed Agile Software Development (DASD)?

Correct answer: C. DASD requires adaptation of Agile ceremonies for geographically dispersed teams.

Question 2: Which of the following is a recommended strategy to enhance team cohesion in a Distributed Agile Software Development (DASD) environment?

Correct answer: C. Use regular virtual team-building activities and cross-location pair programming.
