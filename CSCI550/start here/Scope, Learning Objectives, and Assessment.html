 Course Overview

Scope, Learning Objectives, and Assessment

| Course Scope

The course includes modern software engineering practices, agile methodologies, continuous integration/continuous deployment (CI/CD), and DevSecOps principles. It explores the impact of emerging technologies like Artificial Intelligence (AI), machine learning, and cloud computing on software development. Students will learn to apply advanced design patterns, implement secure coding practices, and utilize cloud-native technologies.

| Program Learning Outcomes

Program Learning Outcomes (PLO) serve as a roadmap for educational success and guide the courses that you take.

This course supports the following PLOs:

  • PLO1 Synthesize and evaluate complex computing problems by integrating advanced computing principles and interdisciplinary approaches to develop innovative solutions.
  • PLO2 Design, develop, and assess comprehensive computing-based solutions, considering scalability, security, and sustainability, within specific domains.
  • PLO3 Explain technical concepts and research findings effectively to diverse audiences, both orally and in writing, demonstrating professional communication skills.
  • PLO4 Integrate ethical and professional responsibility in computing practices, adhering to legal and ethical principles, and considering societal impact.
  • PLO5 Collaborate effectively within diverse teams, fostering a positive team environment and achieving shared objectives through effective project management.
  • PLO6 Synthesize computer science concepts and practical skills to create innovative, high-quality computing solutions that address real-world challenges.
