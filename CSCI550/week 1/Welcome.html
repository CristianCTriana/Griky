What to Expect in Week 1

Welcome to Week One of Advanced Software Engineering and DevSecOps! This foundational week introduces you to the core principles and practices that drive modern software development. You will begin by exploring various Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC) models and their evolution in response to industry demands. Your journey continues with an in-depth look at Agile methodologies, examining how they transform traditional development approaches. The integration of DevSecOps principles will demonstrate how security can be seamlessly woven into the development pipeline. Through practical examples and industry-relevant case studies, you will discover how these methodologies and practices come together to create efficient, secure, and scalable software solutions.

This comprehensive introduction sets the stage for understanding modern software engineering practices while providing practical insights into their implementation in real-world scenarios.

Welcome to Week 1

Transcript: Welcome to Week 1 ▼

This week introduces learners to the foundations of data structures and algorithms, the essential tools behind efficient problem-solving in computer science. They'll explore how arrays, linked lists, stacks, and queues organize information, and why selecting the right structure dramatically impacts an algorithm's speed and memory usage.

Learners will examine Big-O notation to understand how algorithms scale, and they'll practice implementing basic operations in Python to build confidence and fluency.

By the end of the week, they'll see how these structures appear everywhere—from scheduling tasks to managing browser history—and how mastering them lays the groundwork for every advanced technique that follows.
