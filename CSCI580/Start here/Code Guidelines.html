CSCI580 Course Overview

Guidelines

The following highlights items and supports that are unique to this course. It includes technology requirements, academic integrity guidance, Gen-AI usage, and preferred citation style.

| Specific Technology Needs

This course does not require any unique technologies for successful completion.

| Academic Integrity

This course adheres to the Standards of Student Conduct [webpage] as presented in the Student Handbook.

| Generative Artificial Intelligence Usage

This course encourages exploring and utilizing generative artificial intelligence (Gen-AI) tools and resources. Guidelines include:

    • Exploration: Experiment with Gen-AI technologies, algorithms, and frameworks.
    • Academic Integrity: Properly cite any AI-generated content or code.
    • Collaboration: Discuss Gen-AI concepts with peers.
    • Ethical Considerations: Be mindful of societal impact, privacy, and bias.

Review individual assignments for unique Gen-AI guidelines. 
See Gen-AI in the Classroom [content page] for details.

This course utilizes APA [webpage] formatting and citations.

Citation Style requirements do NOT apply in Discussions. Describe sources used, but full citations are not required.

A student should include the following statement in assignments to credit the use of a Generative AI Tool:

"I utilized [Generative AI Tool Name], a Gen-AI system created by [Generative AI Tool Provider], to complete this assignment. [Generative AI Tool Name] was employed for the following purposes: [e.g., ideating concepts, proofreading, formatting citations, assisting with specific sections of the work]."
