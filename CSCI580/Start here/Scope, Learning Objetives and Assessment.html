 Course Overview

Scope, Learning Objectives, and Assessment

| Course Scope

The course provides an in-depth understanding of the architectural concepts behind the design of modern computers. It provides the opportunity to develop the ability to analyze and optimize the performance, efficiency, and scalability of computer architectures and equipped with the skills and knowledge to contribute to the design and improvement of advanced computing architectures.

  • Design, develop, and assess comprehensive computing-based solutions, considering scalability, security, and sustainability, within specific domains.

  • Collaborate effectively within diverse teams, fostering a positive team environment and achieving shared objectives through effective project management.
  • Synthesize computer science concepts and practical skills to create innovative, high-quality computing solutions that address real-world challenges.
