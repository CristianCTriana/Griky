 Course Overview

Scope, Learning Objectives, and Assessment

| Course Scope

Emphasis in this course will be placed on problems and opportunities of business leaders and managers. It will also provide you an opportunity to analyze several components critically, which will challenge you to apply management principles to accomplishing growth in business enterprises. The ultimate objective of the course is to enable the student to evaluate and to solve problems involving business management and to communicate effectively their recommendations to others.
