ISSC661
 Course Overview

Scope, Learning Objectives, and Assessment

| Course Scope

This course is a study of the discipline of Information Assurance as it relates to security assessments and evaluations.

| Course & Learning Objectives

Select each Course Objective to see the associated Learning Objectives and Assessments.

Learning Objectives

  • LO-1.1: Define the core principles of information assurance.
  • LO-1.2: Analyze how risk management frameworks support the protection of digital assets.
  • LO-1.3: Assess the limitations of cryptographic systems in the context of emerging threats.

Assessments

  • Week 1 Discussion: Foundations of Information Assurance 
  • Week 3 Discussion: Cryptography and Technical Safeguards
  • Week 7 - Corporate Cybersecurity Strategy Development

Learning Objectives

  • LO-2.1: Critique the impact of key cybersecurity laws and regulations on corporate security practices.
  • LO-2.2: Assess ethical dilemmas in cybersecurity decision-making. 

Assessments

  • Week 2 Discussion: Organizational, Legal, and Ethical Dimensions 
  • Week 2 - Case Study Analysis: Legal and Ethical Challenges in Corporate Cybersecurity
  • Week 7 - Corporate Cybersecurity Strategy Development
  • Week 8 Discussion: Integration and Future Directions

Learning Objectives

| Program Learning Outcomes

Program Learning Outcomes (PLO) serve as a roadmap for educational success and guide the courses that you take

This course supports the following concentration PLOs:

  • Analyze the methodologies for investigating computer-related crime and for incident handling.
  • Appraise the legal and regulatory compliance requirements related to Information Assurance and Information Systems.
  • Analyze the challenges encountered in establishing information systems security, information assurance, and business continuity.
  • Examine the models and methodologies for performing security vulnerability assessment and risk mitigation; also analyze the principles and practices for appraising and certifying systems security.
