Information security has become an important concept in the modern digital era. Protecting digital assets is no longer optional as organizations increasingly rely on interconnected systems. Security is not simply optional, but a comprehensive practice integrating people, processes, and technology (Death, 2023).

This lesson includes a discussion on the principles of information assurance, the methodologies used to protect digital assets, and the role of risk management in security planning. These elements are the backbone of a resilient security posture able to adapt to evolving threats.
