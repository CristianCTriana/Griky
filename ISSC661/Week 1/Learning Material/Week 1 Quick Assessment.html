Week 1 Quick Assessment

After working through the content this week, check what you have learned in this short, ungraded practice activity. Select the best answer for each question and then check your responses by clicking the “check answer” button

Question 1: Which of the following best describes the purpose of information security in modern organizations?

Correct answer: B. To integrate people, processes, and technology for protection

Question 2: Which example best illustrates the principle of confidentiality?

Correct answer: A. Using encryption to protect patient records in a doctor’s office

Question 3: Which methodology involves using simulated attacks to test how defenses respond under pressure?

Correct answer: B. Penetration testing

Question 4: Which of the following is an example of strengthening access control?

Correct answer: B. Implementing role-based access and multi-factor authentication

Question 5: Why is risk management considered the foundation of an effective information security program?

Correct answer: C. It identifies, assesses, and prioritizes threats to allocate resources efficiently
