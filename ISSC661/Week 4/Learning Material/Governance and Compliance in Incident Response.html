Week 4 Incident Management and Response

Governance and Compliance in Incident Response

Incident response should operate within a governance framework, such as NIST, ISO 27001, and GDPR to reflect legal obligations, reporting requirements, and data protection mandates (Edwards & Weaver, 2024).

Evidence handling must meet legal standards, and incident documentation must support audits and investigations (Johansen, 2025). Governance includes capability oversight to ensure incident response teams are well-trained and equipped and evaluated regularly (Goldschmidt, 2025).

Effective governance ensures that incident response is not just reactive but strategic, accountable, and aligned with organizational risk management.
