Week 5 Convergence of Security and Warfare

Integrating Frameworks into National Strategy

A multi-layered approach that integrates policy, technology, and human expertise is required.

Legal Frameworks

Must evolve to define cybercrime clearly and facilitate international cooperation.

Technical Defenses

Should be standardized and continuously updated.

Human Factors

Such as training, awareness, and behavior, must be prioritized (Ievdokymov, 2024).

A blueprint for designing secure, adaptable information systems should emphasize modularity, cooperation, and confrontation, aligning technological innovation with strategic planning (Bobrova et al., 2024).

  • Governments should establish dedicated cybersecurity agencies, fund research, and foster public-private partnerships.
  • International treaties and joint task forces can address the transnational nature of cyber threats.
  • Lastly, education systems must incorporate cybersecurity literacy to prepare future generations for these threats.
