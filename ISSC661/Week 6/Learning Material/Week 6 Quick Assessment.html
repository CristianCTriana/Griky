Week 6 Quick Assessment

After working through the content this week, check what you have learned in this short, ungraded practice activity. Select the best answer for each question and then check your responses by clicking the “check answer” button.

Question 1: Which of the following is a risk-based cybersecurity framework that emphasizes flexibility and continuous improvement?

Correct answer: C. NIST Cybersecurity Framework (CSF)

Question 2: ISO/IEC 27001 requires formal certification, while NIST CSF allows self-assessment.

Correct answer: True

Question 3: Which U.S. federal regulation encourages public-private collaboration by facilitating threat intelligence sharing?

Correct answer: C. CISA

Question 4: Under California’s Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), the Attorney General can impose fines of:

Correct answer: B. $2,500 per violation, escalating to $7,500 for intentional breaches

Question 5: Which international regulation applies to any organization processing personal data of EU residents, regardless of location, and can impose fines up to €20 million?

Correct answer: B. GDPR (European Union)
