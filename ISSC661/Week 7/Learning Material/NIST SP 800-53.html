NIST SP 800-53

The NIST Special Publication 800-53 is a comprehensive catalog of security and privacy controls used primarily by federal agencies and contractors. It supports the RMF by providing detailed guidance on selecting and implementing controls based on system impact levels.

SP 800-53 is organized into control families such as Access Control, Audit and Accountability, Incident Response, and System and Communications Protection.

Each control family contains individual controls, which are further broken down into control enhancements. These enhancements allow for tailoring based on specific threats, technologies, and organizational needs.

Privacy Integration

SP 800-53 also includes privacy controls, recognizing the growing importance of data protection and regulatory compliance. These controls align with laws such as the Privacy Act and the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

By integrating security and privacy, SP 800-53 supports a holistic approach to risk management.

The publication is updated regularly to reflect emerging threats, technologies, and regulatory changes. It is important to use the latest revision to ensure relevance and compliance. The controls are designed to be technology-neutral, enabling implementation across diverse environments, from cloud platforms to legacy systems.
