Week 8 Integration and Future Directions

GenAI’s Role in Cyber Threat Detection and Response

GenAI enhances cybersecurity by enabling systems to learn from vast datasets and generate predictive models that anticipate threats before they occur. GenAI can simulate attack scenarios, identify vulnerabilities, and automate responses with unprecedented speed and accuracy (Islam, 2024).

Unlike traditional AI, GenAI does not just classify or detect, it creates synthetic data and scenarios that enrich training models and improve resilience.

One of GenAI’s most promising applications is in threat intelligence.

Proactive Preparation

By generating realistic phishing emails, malware variants, or network traffic patterns, GenAI helps security teams prepare for emerging threats (Islam, 2024).

Automated Incident Response

It also supports automated incident response, where AI-generated playbooks guide systems through containment and recovery steps. This reduces human error and accelerates mitigation.

Risks & Malicious Use

However, GenAI’s power also introduces risks (Islam, 2024). Malicious actors can use GenAI to craft sophisticated attacks, such as deepfake social engineering or polymorphic malware.

Therefore, cybersecurity professionals must not only harness GenAI’s capabilities but also anticipate its misuse.

Ethical frameworks and governance models are essential to ensure responsible deployment.
