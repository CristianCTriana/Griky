One key area of concern is data governance. GenAI systems often rely on massive datasets, which may include sensitive, proprietary, or personally identifiable information. Policies that ensure transparency in data sourcing, consent management, and usage boundaries are needed (Islam, 2024).

Regulatory bodies must enforce compliance with data protection laws such as GDPR and CCPA, while also adapting to the unique challenges posed by synthetic data generation.
