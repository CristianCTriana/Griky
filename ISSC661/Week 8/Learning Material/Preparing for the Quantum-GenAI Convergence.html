Week 8 Integration and Future Directions

Preparing for the Quantum-GenAI Convergence

The convergence of GenAI and quantum computing presents both opportunities and risks. These technologies could complement each other in powerful ways.

GenAI → Quantum

GenAI can help simulate quantum algorithms, optimize quantum circuits, and interpret quantum data (Islam, 2024).

Quantum → GenAI

Quantum computing, in turn, could accelerate GenAI training and inference (Edwards & Weaver, 2024).

However, this convergence also amplifies risks.

  • GenAI could be used to design quantum attacks or automate quantum cryptanalysis.
  • Quantum computing could enable faster generation of deepfakes or synthetic threats.

The combined power of these technologies demands robust safeguards and interdisciplinary oversight.
