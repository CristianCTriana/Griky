MGMT605 Course Overview

Questions for the Instructor

As you progress through the class, there is a Q&A Discussion that allows you to talk to each other and ask the instructor questions.

  • If you have a non-personal question about the course, please post it in the Q&A Discussion. Other students will be able to see your question and the instructor's response. So, if you have a question that can benefit the entire class, go for it!
  • If you have a question that is more sensitive in nature, please utilize the 1:1 Chat with Prof, located on your course homepage and in the discussions.

The Q&A Discussion [course webpage] is located in the Discussions area.
