Leadership Styles, Motivation, and Productivity

Leadership style is not one-size-fits-all.

Hasanuddin and Syamsuddin (2025) highlight how transformational, transactional, and participative leadership styles affect employee motivation and organizational productivity. Transformational leaders inspire through vision and personal connection, while transactional leaders focus on structure and rewards. Participative leaders engage employees in decision-making, fostering ownership and commitment.

The work environment also plays a crucial role. A supportive, inclusive, and well-resourced environment enhances the impact of leadership and boosts motivation. When employees feel valued and empowered, productivity rises.
