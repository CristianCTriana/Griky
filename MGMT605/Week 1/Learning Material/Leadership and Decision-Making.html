Leadership and Decision-Making

Learning Challenge

ⓘ This is not an assignment prompt, but rather an opportunity to reflect on how the week’s concepts apply to real workplace situations.

Decision-making is a cornerstone of organizational success, and leadership plays a pivotal role in shaping how decisions are made.

According to Krasniqi et al. (2024), leadership behaviors such as active listening, ethical reasoning, and emotional intelligence significantly influence the quality and effectiveness of decisions.
Leaders who foster open communication and psychological safety empower teams to contribute ideas and challenge assumptions. This inclusive approach leads to more informed, creative, and sustainable decisions. Conversely, autocratic or disengaged leadership can stifle innovation and lead to poor outcomes.
