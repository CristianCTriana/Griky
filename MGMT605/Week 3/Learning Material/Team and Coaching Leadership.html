How Leaders Inspire Vision

Team and Coaching Leadership

Team leadership emphasizes collaboration, shared purpose, and collective goal achievement. Leaders who adopt this style are guided by a clear vision and strong values, and they actively engage team members by tapping into their strengths, ideas, and motivations.

Key skills associated with team leadership include motivating others, fostering participation, and using persuasion to align efforts with organizational objectives. Similar to democratic leadership, team leaders often share responsibility and decision-making authority. While this inclusive approach can enhance engagement and innovation, it may also present challenges in achieving consensus or maintaining direction.

Notable examples of team leaders include:
  • Sheryl Sandberg, former COO of Meta (Facebook), known for promoting collaborative leadership
  • Tony Hsieh, former CEO of Zappos, who emphasized team culture and empowerment
  • Pat Summitt, legendary women's basketball coach, recognized for her team-centered leadership approach

Coaching leadership is centered on developing individuals within an organization by teaching, motivating, and guiding them toward personal and professional growth. Leaders who adopt this style focus on enhancing the skills of their team members and helping them achieve specific goals through encouragement, constructive feedback, and ongoing support.

This approach is commonly seen in team environments where collaboration and performance improvement are essential. Coaching leaders are known for their ability to foster trust, build confidence, and create a culture of continuous learning.

Key traits of coaching leadership include:
  • Providing meaningful feedback
  • Encouraging participation and growth
  • Motivating individuals to reach their potential
Notable examples of coaching leaders include:
  • Phil Jackson, former NBA coach known for developing championship teams
  • Oprah Winfrey, recognized for mentoring and empowering others through media and philanthropy
  • Eric Schmidt, former CEO of Google, known for nurturing talent and guiding leadership development
