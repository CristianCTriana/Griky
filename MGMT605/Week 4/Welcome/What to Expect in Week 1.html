What to Expect in Week 4

The models for leading change give us a lens through which we can see our personal situation in a new light. They help us to develop our mental map of the world―whether that concerns a situation, project team, or organization―and create options for dealing with those difficult circumstances we inevitably encounter in our daily lives
Common themes from each model are:
  • Self-awareness
  • Self-management
  • Social awareness
  • Relationship management
