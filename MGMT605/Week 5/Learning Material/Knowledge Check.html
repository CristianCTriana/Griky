How Leadership Relates to Teamwork


Week 5 - Knowledge Check

Question: You are leading a cross-functional team that is struggling with collaboration and motivation. Based on the key elements of leadership strategy that influence teamwork, which of the following actions would most effectively strengthen team cohesion and performance?

Correct answer: B. Provide regular, constructive feedback and recognize individual contributions.

Explanation: Providing regular feedback and recognizing individual efforts fosters accountability, motivation, and a sense of belonging. These are essential for building trust and encouraging teamwork. Decision-making, vision alignment, and recognition are all leadership strategies that contribute to a collaborative and high-performing team.
