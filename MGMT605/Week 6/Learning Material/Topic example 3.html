Week # Assessing Organizational Strategy

Topic name 3

To develop, implement, and sustain its strategies, an organization must have a good process of strategic management, and that process should include an effective planning function.

These details include who will do the tasks necessary to complete each activity, how they will do them, and the deadlines for completion. Plans also should provide details about alternative courses of action that should be taken if a preferred course of action must be changed for some reason. When managers plan, they also determine the resources needed to carry out the tasks required to meet an organization’s goals and objectives, and they set the standards that must be met to achieve the goals and objectives.
