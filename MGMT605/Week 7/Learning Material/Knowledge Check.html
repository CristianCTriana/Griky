Week 7 - Knowledge Check

Scenario: 

You are a team leader trying to implement an employee empowerment initiative. Despite clear benefits, you notice resistance from both leadership and staff. Based on the common obstacles to empowerment, answer the following multiple-choice question: 

Question: Which of the following strategies would best help overcome a common barrier to employee empowerment?

Correct answer: C. Clearly define empowerment expectations and tailor them to your team’s environment.

Explanation: One major barrier to empowerment is the lack of clear goals and over-reliance on generic programs. Empowerment must be tailored to the specific work environment, and leaders should set clear, actionable goals to guide employees. This helps build trust, motivation, and consistency.
