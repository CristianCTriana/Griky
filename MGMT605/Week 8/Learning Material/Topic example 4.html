Assessing Organizational Strategy

Topic name 4

Organizations strive to stay in existence for a long period. Long-range planning is important to help achieve this.

A long-range plan states the organization’s primary goals that will be achieved in the future and outlines the direction the organization will take to achieve these goals. Long-range plans analyze the organization's current status and estimate what the organization will need to do to reach its long-term goals.

Strategic planning is practiced to supplement the long-range plan and can be defined as the process of determining the major goals of the organization and the policies and strategies for obtaining and using resources to achieve those goals.

(Nickels et al. 185)
