What to Expect in Week 8

In our final week, we bring together the themes and skills explored throughout the course to examine leadership in action. Imagine a leader navigating a global crisis, balancing ethical decision-making, team morale, and strategic direction. This leader must draw on a personal philosophy, theoretical models, and DEIB principles to guide their actions.
Brooks et al. (2024) highlight the importance of character and leadership education in preparing future leaders. Edvardsson & Gardarsdottir (2023) explore the challenges of telework and crisis leadership during COVID-19. Ouakouak et al. (2025) emphasize the role of a positive mindset in organizational resilience. Walker (2023) provides a concise overview of executive leadership fundamentals.
This week, you will reflect on your leadership journey, apply course concepts to real-world scenarios, and articulate your personal leadership philosophy.
