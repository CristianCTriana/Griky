Leaders vs. Managers

Leadership vs. Management: Understanding the Divide

Leadership and management are often used interchangeably, but they represent distinct roles.

Managers
Managers focus on systems, processes, and control. They ensure stability and efficiency.
Leaders
Leaders, on the other hand, inspire vision, drive change, and cultivate innovation.
Reeder (2023) emphasizes that recognizing this difference allows organizations to harness the strengths of both roles. Sinek (2024) adds that leadership is about people, while management is about tasks.
